What kind of stuff?

Newsletters, direct mail, press releases, brochures, websites and every kind of advert - I've written copy to create new brands and to help increase the sales of established products. For the past 4 years, I've been practicing creating lighthearted, fun and effective messaging for an in-house marketing department, specialising in information products.

Now I'm a full-time writer, trying to make companies sound more human, one bit of copy at a time. If you'd like to see the kind of thing I mean, bounce on down to my portfolio. And tell me what you think.
